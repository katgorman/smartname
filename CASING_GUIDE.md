# CASING_GUIDE.md

This guide explains the six file naming styles supported by **SmartName**.

---

### 1. snake_case (default)
**Format:** lowercase words separated by underscores.  

Example:  
`machine_learning_notes.pdf`

---

### 2. kebab-case
**Format:** lowercase words separated by hyphens.  

Example:  
`final-design-assets.zip`

---

### 3. camelCase
**Format:** first word lowercase, each following word capitalized.  

Example:  
`projectOverviewSlides.pptx`

---

### 4. PascalCase
**Format:** each word capitalized (no spaces).  

Example:  
`DataCollectionReport.docx`

---

### 5. lowercase with spaces
**Format:** all lowercase, spaces between words.  

Example:  
`meeting notes january.txt`

---

### 6. Title Case With Spaces
**Format:** each word capitalized, spaces between words.  

Example:  
`AI Ethics Paper.docx`
